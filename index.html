                Create a detailed normal map from this cat image. Emphasize the following surface details with accurate depth information:

                FACIAL FEATURES:
                - Subtle fur texture across the face with directional flow
                - Raised nose bridge and nostril depth
                - Eye socket depth and spherical curvature of the eyeballs
                - Individual whiskers as thin raised elements
                - Ear contours with inner concave surfaces

                FUR TEXTURE:
                - Fine short fur texture on face and body
                - Directional fur flow patterns
                - Slightly longer chest fur with more pronounced texture
                - Variation in fur density between smooth and fluffy areas

                BODY GEOMETRY:
                - Rounded head and facial structure
                - Muscular legs with subtle anatomical definition
                - Paw pads as slightly raised soft surfaces
                - Body contours showing natural cat anatomy

                OBJECT SURFACE:
                - Smooth plastic texture of the green stool
                - Gentle curves of the stool seat
                - Central hole with depth

                OUTPUT SPECS:
                - RGB normal map format (red=X, green=Y, blue=Z)
                - Medium-high detail level appropriate for 3D rendering
                - Balanced depth - not too flat, not overly exaggerated
                - Clean edges between cat silhouette and background
                - Neutral (0.5, 0.5, 1.0 / 128, 128, 255) for flat areas
            
        
            
                Create a clean albedo (diffuse color) map from this cat image. Remove all lighting, shadows, and specular highlights to show only pure surface colors:

                COLOR INFORMATION TO PRESERVE:
                - Gray-brown fur color on head, back, and body
                - White fur on face (bridge of nose, muzzle, chin)
                - White chest fur
                - White paws/feet
                - Pink nose leather
                - Pink inner mouth/lips
                - Natural eye color (greenish-yellow irises, black pupils)
                - White whiskers
                - Teal/green stool base color

                CORRECTIONS TO APPLY:
                - Remove all shadows (under chin, between legs, ear shadows)
                - Eliminate highlights (on nose, eyes, stool surface)
                - Flatten lighting to show uniform base colors
                - Remove ambient occlusion darkening
                - Eliminate reflected light color casts
                - Normalize exposure across entire surface

                TEXTURE DETAILS TO MAINTAIN:
                - Subtle fur color variation (not lighting-based)
                - Natural color transitions between gray and white areas
                - Slight color variation in plastic stool (material color only)
                - Keep individual fur strand color variety
                - Preserve natural markings and patterns

                OUTPUT SPECS:
                - Pure diffuse color only - no lighting information
                - Even, flat appearance as if lit by 100% neutral white light
                - No black crush or white blowout
                - Mid-tone values preserved
                - Clean, game-ready albedo texture
                - Background should be neutral or transparent

                The result should look like a material swatch - showing what color the surfaces ARE, not how they're LIT.
            
        
            
                Create a grayscale specular/glossiness map from this cat image, where white = very shiny/specular, gray = slightly specular, and black = no specular reflection.

                SPECULAR ASSIGNMENTS:

                HIGH SPECULAR (WHITE - bright values 200-255):
                - Cat eyes (cornea/wet surface of eyeballs) - very reflective
                - Eye highlights area - maximum specularity
                - Nose leather (wet appearance) - high specular

                MEDIUM SPECULAR (MID-GRAY - values 100-150):
                - Plastic stool surface - moderate shine
                - Paw pads (slightly moist) - subtle specular

                LOW SPECULAR (DARK GRAY - values 30-80):
                - Short fur on face - minimal specular
                - Body fur - very subtle shine
                - Whiskers - slight specular

                NO SPECULAR (BLACK - values 0-20):
                - Fluffy/matte fur on chest - completely matte
                - Longer fur areas - no specular
                - Inner ears - matte
                - White facial fur - matte
                - Background - pure black

                KEY REQUIREMENTS:
                - Eyes should be the brightest/whitest area (primary specular hotspot)
                - Nose should be bright but slightly less than eyes
                - Most of the cat's fur should be very dark (nearly black) - fur is matte
                - Stool should be uniform medium gray (plastic has moderate shine)
                - Sharp, clean transitions between specular zones
                - No color information - pure grayscale only
                - Smooth gradients where appropriate (like on eye spheres)

                OUTPUT: Grayscale map optimized for PBR specular workflow
